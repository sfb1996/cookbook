# Instructions
Mix and chill overnight:
- [ ] 2 cups fresh cranberries, ground (measure when whole)
- [ ] 2 cups tiny marshmallows
- [ ] 3/4 cup sugar

Before serving, add:
- [ ] 2 cups diced apples
- [ ] 1/2 cup chopped waltnuts
- [ ] 1/4 tsp salt
- [ ] 1/2 cup green or red seedless grapes

Mix.

Fold in 1 cup whipped cream.
