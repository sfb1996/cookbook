Blend:  
- [ ] 1 cup butter 
- [ ] 1 cup sugar 
- [ ] 1 cup brown sugar
- [ ] 2 eggs, beaten

Combine and add to above:
- [ ] 1/2 tsp.  baking powder
- [ ] 1 tsp vanilla
- [ ] 1/2 tsp salt
- [ ] 1 tsp soda + 1/2 tsp.  warm water

Add:
- [ ] 2-1/2 cups quick oats
- [ ] 2-1/4 cups flour
- [ ] one 12-oz.  pkg chocolate chips
- [ ] 1 cup coconut

Mix well. Make 1-inch balls.

Bake at 400 for 5-7 minutes
