# Ingredients:
- [ ] 4 1/2 cups sugar
- [ ] 1 3/4 cups chocolate chips
- [ ] 1 large evaporated milk (13 oz)	
- [ ] 10 1/2 oz. Hershey bar
- [ ] 1 cube (1/4 lb) butter (or marg)
- [ ] 1 pint marshmallow cream
- [ ] 2 tsp vanilla
- [ ] 2 cups chopped nuts

# Instructions
1. Bring sugar, milk, and butter to a boil, stirring constantly.
2. Let boil 7-7 1/2 minutes, stirring occasionally.
3. Pour over mixture of marshmallow creme, chocolate bar, and chips.
4. Stir until melted.
5. Add nuts and vanilla.
6. Pour into 9 x 13 buttered pan.
7. Chill.  
 
*Makes 5 pounds.*
