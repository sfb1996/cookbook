# Ingredients
**Dip:**
- [ ] 1 Tbsp. chopped green chilies
- [ ] 3/4 cups chopped onion
- [ ] 1 chopped tomato
- [ ] 10 oz. frozen spinach
- [ ] 8 oz. cream cheese
- [ ] 2 cups Jack cheese
- [ ] 1/3 cups half & half

**Pitas:**
- [ ] 6 pitas
- [ ] 2 tsp. ground cumin
- [ ] 2 tsp. lemon pepper
- [ ] 1/2 cups margarine, melted

# Instructions
1. Thaw and drain the Spinach.  Combine all the dip ingredients.  Pour into a buttered ovenproof dish. Bake at 400Â° for 20 to 25 minutes
2. Slice pitas open and cut into triangles. Dip into the lemon pepper, cumin, and margarine mixture and then broil until crispy.
