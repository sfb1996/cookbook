# Ingredients:
- [ ] 1 cup flour
- [ ] 1/2 cup milk
- [ ] 1/2 cup water
- [ ] 1/4 tsp salt
- [ ] 2 eggs

# Instructions
1. Preheat oven to 425 °F.  Add ½ tsp oil to each well in a 12-serving muffin tin and heat for 15 min.
2. Mix all ingredients with a hand mixer until smooth.  Pour batter into all 12 holders (oil should sizzle).
3. Bake for approximately 25 minutes, or until golden brown and risen.
