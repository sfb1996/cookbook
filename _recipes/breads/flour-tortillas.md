# Ingredients
- [ ] 2 cups flour
- [ ] 1 tsp salt
- [ ] 1/4 cup oil

1. Mix all ingredients.
2. Add 1/2 - 3/4 cup HOT water (all at once).
3. Let rest.
4. Roll out & cook on an ungreased griddle/pan until slightly golden and cooked through.
