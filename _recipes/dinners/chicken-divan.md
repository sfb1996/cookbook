# Ingredients:
## Main Contents
 - [ ] 3 chicken breasts
 - [ ] 2 10oz packages broccoli

 ## Sauce
 - [ ] 1 can Cream of Chicken soup
 - [ ] 2/3 cup mayonnaise
 - [ ] 1/3 cup evaporated milk
 - [ ] 1 cup grated cheese
 - [ ] 1 tsp lemon juice
 - [ ] 1/2 tsp curry powder

 ## Topping
 - [ ] 1/2 cup fine dry break crumbs
 - [ ] 1 tbsp soft margarine

# Instructions

1. Cook chicken breasts and bone, if necessary. Cut into bite-sized pieces or shred. Set aside.
   - *Tip: we prefer to cook the chicken in an Instant Pot using the pressure cook setting. Pressure cook for about 30-35 minutes.*
2. Cook broccoli until almost done. Set aside.
   - *Tip: we prefer to [steam the broccoli](https://www.loveandlemons.com/steamed-broccoli/).*
3. Mix together ingredients for sauce: Cream of Chicken, mayonnaise, evaporated milk, grated cheese, lemon juice and curry powder.
4. Mix sauce, broccoli, and chicken in a large bowl. Spread into a 9x13 pan.
5. Mix bread crumbs with margarine. Top the 9x13 pan contents with the bread crumb / margarine mixture.
6. Bake at 350 degrees F for 25-30 minutes.
