# Ingredients
- [ ] 1 pound bacon, uncooked
- [ ] 6 ounces (1/2 package) bowtie pasta
- [ ] 2 C pecans, chopped roughly
- [ ] 3 C chopped broccoli florets
- [ ] 3 C red grapes
- [ ] 1/4 C red onion, chopped

## Dressing:
- [ ] 1 C mayonnaise
- [ ] 1/2 C apple cider vinegar
- [ ] 1/4 C sugar
- [ ] 1 t salt
- [ ] 2 T bacon grease


1. Cook bacon and transfer to a plate lined with paper towels. Reserve 2 Tbs bacon grease to use in the dressing.
2. Cook 6 oz. bowtie pasta noodles. Once cooked, strain and run cold water over the noodles until cool.
3. Toss chopped pecans into a large skillet and sprinkle a bit of salt over the top. Toast over medium high heat until pecans change color and smell fragrant (2-3 minutes).
4. To a large bowl, add broccoli, grapes, and red onion.
5. Crumble bacon and add to bowl.
6. Add toasted pecans and pasta.  Stir.
7. In a small mixing bowl, combine mayonnaise, apple cider vinegar, sugar, salt and bacon grease. Whisk to combine. 
8. Pour the dressing over the top of the salad and stir it with a big wooden spoon, making sure all of the salad ingredients are well coated.
