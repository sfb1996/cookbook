# Ingredients
- [ ] 2 packages manicotti
- [ ] 1 lb lean ground beef
- [ ] 2 eggs
- [ ] 1 box chicken stuffing mix
- [ ] 2 cups cottage cheese
- [ ] 2 cups cheddar cheese, shredded
- [ ] 1/8 cup Parmesan cheese, grated
- [ ] 8 cups spaghetti sauce, prepared

# Instructions
1. Spray 2 9x14 or 3 8x8 pans.
2. Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
3. In a large bowl, mix dry stuffing mix and seasoning packet, ground beef, eggs, cottage cheese, Parmesan cheese, and cheddar cheese to make a thick paste.
4. Set mixture aside for 5 minutes so bread crumbs can absorb moisture.
5. Begin stuffing uncooked manicotti shells by hand; place them side-by-side into prepared baking pans. Continue until all shells have been filled and are placed as desired into baking pans.
6. Spoon spaghetti sauce evenly over shells until all of the sauce has been distributed.
7. Cover with foil and bake for 2 hours.

---

*To Freeze*: Cover with foil tightly and mark each package. Good for up to 6 months. To bake, either:
- Thaw overnight in refrigerator and bake as normal.
- Bake from frozen at 275 for 1 hr followed by 1.5 hours at 350.
