# Ingredients
- [ ] 1 pound sliced bacon, diced
- [ ] 1 medium sweet onion, chopped (2/3)
- [ ] 6 eggs, lightly beaten (8)
- [ ] 4 cups frozen shredded hash brown potatoes, thawed
- [ ] 2 cups (8 ounces) shredded cheddar cheese
- [ ] 1-1/2 cups (12 ounces) small-curd cottage cheese
- [ ] 1-1/4 cups shredded Swiss cheese

# Instructions
1. In a large skillet, cook bacon and onion until bacon is crisp; drain. 
2. In a bowl, combine the remaining ingredients; stir in bacon mixture. Transfer to a greased 13-in. x 9-in. x 2-in. baking dish. 
3. Bake, uncovered, at 350Â° for 35-40 minutes or until set and bubbly. Let stand for 10 minutes before cutting.

*Yield: 12 servings.*
