# Ingredients
- [ ] Diced cooked chicken - 2-1/2 cups (~ 3 chicken breasts)
- [ ] 1-3/4 cups diced celery
- [ ] 1 tsp salt
- [ ] 1/4 tsp pepper
- [ ] 3-4 cups cooked rice (two cups water, one cup rice, cooked in bouillon)
- [ ] 2 cans cream of chicken soup
- [ ] 1-1/2 Tbsp chopped green onions (~4 green onions)
- [ ] Diced green peppers (optional)
- [ ] 2-5 diced hard-boiled eggs
- [ ] 1-1/2 cups mayonnaise
- [ ] ranch dressing to add flavor (optional)
- [ ] grated cheese (optional)
- [ ] 1-1/2 tsp.  lemon juice (optional)

# Instructions
1. Mix ingredients, put in buttered pan.
2. Topping: crushed pepperidge farm dressing with butter OR crushed potato chips.
3. Bake at 350 for 30-45 minutes.
