# Ingredients:
- [ ] 4 cup fusilli noodles
- [ ] 1/2 cup mayonnaise
- [ ] 1/3 cup ranch dressing
- [ ] 4 green onions, (chopped)
- [ ] 6 strips  bacon, (cooked and crumbled)
- [ ] 1/2 teaspoon garlic salt, (or to taste)
- [ ] 1/2 Tablespoon onion powder, (or to taste)
- [ ] 1/2 teaspoon pepper
- [ ] 1 1/2 cup shredded cheddar cheese

# Instructions
1. Cook noodles according to package directions.
2. In a large mixing bowl, combine the mayonnaise, ranch dressing, green onions, bacon, garlic salt, onion powder, and pepper until well combined.
3. Add your cooled noodles and cheddar cheese.
4. Mix until well combined. Place in fridge for about 2 hours before serving.

---

# Notes

- Store the pasta salad in an airtight container in the fridge until ready to serve! Any leftovers can be stored in the refrigerator 5 days.
- Homemade ranch makes this recipe even better! Make sure you don’t overcook the pasta and wait to add all the bacon until just before serving so that it’s still crispy.
- Let the pasta cool just a bit before adding the creamy dressing, you want warm, not hot pasta.
- Additional toppings for this Cheesy Bacon Ranch Pasta Salad include the following: black olives, red onions, garlic powder, greek yogurt (in place of mayonnaise), cherry tomoates, sliced chicken breasts, fresh herbs, or grape tomatoes.
