# Ingredients
## Vegetable Step
- [ ] 3 Tbs butter
- [ ] 6 stalks celery, diced
- [ ] 6 carrots, sliced
- [ ] 1.5 onions, diced
- [ ] 3 red potatoes, diced

## Broth Step
- [ ] 1 tbsp minced garlic
- [ ] Salt and pepper to taste
- [ ] 3/8 cup flour
- [ ] 3 cups chicken broth
- [ ] 1.5 8-oz. package cream cheese

## Chicken Step
- [ ] 3 cups rotisserie chicken, shredded
- [ ] 3/4 cup frozen peas

## Pastry Step
- [ ] 1 package puff pastry (~17.3 oz.), thawed
- [ ] 1 egg
- [ ] 2 Tbsp fresh parsley, roughly chopped

# Instructions
1. Preheat oven to 400 F.
2. Melt butter in a large saucepan over medium-high heat.
3. Add celery, carrots, onion, and potatoes. Saut√© 8-10 minutes or until vegetables begin to soften.
4. Add garlic and season with salt and pepper.
5. Mix in flour, chicken broth, and cream cheese. Let cook for 5 minutes, stirring constantly.
6. Fold in chicken and peas.
7. Pour mixture into a baking sheet sprayed with non-stick cooking spray.
8. Roll out pastry sheet(s) to the width of the sheet pan. Cut into 1-inch strips and lay over filling. Cut away any excess dough.
9. Whisk egg in a small bowl and brush over the top of the pot pie.
10. Cook 30-35 minutes or until the top is golden brown.
11. Garnish with fresh parsley and serve.
