*It easy to make several of these as a meal-prep. The ingredients are sized for one 16oz mason jar which is good for one breakfast.*

# Ingredients
- [ ] 1/2 cut rolled oats
- [ ] 1 tablespoon chia seeds
- [ ] 1 teaspoon cinnamon
- [ ] 1/8 teaspoon of salt
- [ ] 1/2 cut milk
- [ ] 1/4 cup of low-fat Greek yogurt (plain preferred but vanilla works - it's just sweeter)
- [ ] 1 tablespoon maple syrup
- [ ] 1/2 peach - about 1/2 cup (frozen works)

# Instructions
1. Add all of the ingredients into a 16oz mason jar and stir well.

---

# Notes
- Storage: Refrigerate for up to four days.
- Milk: Use your preferred milk or milk substitute here, almond, oat, cashew, soy, or cow’s all work! A non-dairy option like unsweetened almond or oat milk is recommended.
