*Makes 4 dozen cookies*

# Instructions
## Part 1
1. In a large, heavy saucepan over low heat, cook butter, brown sugar and water until melted.
2. Add chocolate chips and stir until partially melted.
3. Remove from heat and continue to stir until all the chips are melted.
4. Pour into a large bowl and let cool for 10 minutes.
5. With mixer, beat in eggs one at a time. Add dry ingredient, beating until blended.
6. Chill the dough for one hour.
<br/>
<br/>

## Part 2
1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
2. Line cookie sheet with foil. 
3. Roll teaspoon-sized balls of dough about one inch in diagmeter. 
4. Bake 10-12 minutes (cookies will crisp as they cool).
5. Remove from oven and immediately place a mint on each hot cookie. Allow mint to soften and swirl with the back of a spoon.
6. Cool thoroughly.
