# Ingredients:
## Batter
- [ ] 1/2 butter or margarine, softened
- [ ] 1/2 cup sugar
- [ ] 1 egg
- [ ] 1 cup all-purpose flour
- [ ] 1 1/2 tsp baking powder
- [ ] 1/4 tsp salt
- [ ] 1/2 cup milk
- [ ] 2 cups blueberries, fresh or frozen

## Topping
- [ ] 1/4 cup butter or margarine, softened
- [ ] 1 cup sugar
- [ ] 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
- [ ] 1/2 tsp cinnamon

# Instructions

1. Spray a 9-in square baking pan with non-stick spray. Set aside.
2. Preheat the oven to 375.

**For Batter**

{:start="3"}
3. With electric mixer on medium speed, beat butter and sugar 3 minutes until light and fluffy.
4. Add egg and beat 1 minute more.
5. In a separate bowl, combine flour, baking powder, and salt.
6. Alternate adding combined dry ingredients and milk to butter mixture in 4 batches.
7. Turn into prepared pan and top evenly with blueberries

**For Topping**

{:start="8"}
8. Combine butter, sugar, flour, and cinnamon until crumbly.
9. Sprinkle over blueberries

{:start="10"}
10. Bake for 45 minutes or until cake tests done.
